Welcome to Giok / 기억!

When it comes to learning new things, re-reading notes is super boring and actually very ineffective at creating long-lasting memory connections in your brain.

This means you can spend hours or even days reading notes, just to forget them within a week.

Using active recall and spaced repetition is scientifically proven to not only increase how much you learn, but also help knowledge stick with you for much longer!

This is why we built Giok! Giok, or 기억, is Korean for memory, and is an easy-to-use app designed to help you remember things quicker and retain them longer.
